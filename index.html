Jennifer Snow

I am Jennifer Snow and I am an architect.

I have always been facinated by buildings, their history and their design.
A building is so much more than just a composition of materials; it is a story.

Here is my story.

My name is Jennifer Snow, I am 28 years old and born and raised in Oslo. My love for drawing and designing, as well as my passion for numbers, started already in the 1th grade.
Growing up, I thought;
why not combine the two?

From here, my dream of being an architect came to life. I would say I am inspired by the modernistic and minimalistic architecture. In Aesthetics of Modern Architecture it is written about the motto of architect Ludwig Mies van der Rohe; Less is more.
I agree with that, and this motto is something I keep in the back of my head when I am working.

Being able to work with what I love is a blessing. In this portfolio you will get to know me, my resume and my work a little bit better.

When I am not working, I love to spend my time:

  • Drawing and painting
  • Listening to music
  • Hiking in the mountains
  • Traveling the world
  • Gardening
