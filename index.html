Jennifer Snow

I am Jennifer Snow and I am an architect.

I have always been facinated by buildings, their history and their design.
A building is so much more than just a composition of materials; it is a story.

Here is my story.

My name is Jennifer Snow, I am 28 years old and born and raised in Oslo. My love for drawing and designing, as well as my passion for numbers, started already in the 1th grade.
Growing up my passion got stronger and I eventually thought;
why not combine the two?

My dream of being an architect came to life. My work is inspired by modernistic and minimalistic architecture. In Aesthetics of Modern Architecture the motto Less is more (Ludwig Mies van der Rohe) is menitioned. I agree with that, and this is something I keep in the back of my head when I am working.

Being able to work with what I love is a blessing. In this portfolio you will get to know my work and resume a little bit better. But first I want you to know me a little bit better.

When I am not working I like to spend my time cherishing my hobbies. That includes:

  • Drawing and painting
  • Listening to music
    • My favorite artist is Frank Ocean.This is my favorite album.
  • Hiking in the mountains
  • Traveling the world
    • I love to visit new cities with exiting architecture!
  • Gardening
